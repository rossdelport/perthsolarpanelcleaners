Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: June 2026

Perth Solar Panel Cleaners ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and customers who use our services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your personal information, in line with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

Information we collect

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to provide our solar panel cleaning, gutter cleaning and pigeon proofing services. This may include:

  • Your name;
  • Your phone number and email address;
  • Your suburb or service address;
  • Details about the service you are requesting (e.g. number of panels, property type); and
  • Any other information you choose to provide in your message.

How we collect it

We collect this information directly from you when you complete our "Get a Free Quote" form, call or text us, email us, or otherwise contact us about our services.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and prepare a quote;
  • Arrange, schedule and carry out the services you request;
  • Contact you about your booking or service; and
  • Improve our services and customer experience.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We will not use your details for marketing unrelated to your enquiry without your consent.

Disclosure to third parties

Our quote form is delivered to us using a third-party form-handling service, which transmits your message to our email inbox. We may also use reputable third-party providers for email and hosting. These providers only process your information for the purpose of operating our website and delivering your enquiry to us.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies & analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Access & correction

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct it if it is inaccurate, by contacting us using the details below.
