Our story

Honest work, sparkling results

Perth's sun is brilliant for solar — but our dust, sea salt, pollen and bird droppings build up fast and quietly steal your output. We started Perth Solar Panel Cleaners because too many local systems were underperforming simply because nobody was keeping them clean.

Today we help homeowners and businesses right across Perth get the most from their investment. Regular cleaning can lift efficiency and electricity production by 15–25% — money straight back in your pocket and more of the clean energy you paid for.

We treat every roof like our own. We turn up when we say we will, give you a fair upfront price, and we don't pack up until the job is done properly. That's our "cleaned until you're happy" promise.
