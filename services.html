Our cleaning services

Everything your rooftop needs to keep performing — pure-water solar panel cleaning, professional gutter clearing, and long-lasting pigeon proofing. Safe, thorough and backed by our satisfaction promise.

Service 01

Solar Panel Cleaning

Perth's dust, sea salt, pollen and bird droppings form a film over your panels that blocks sunlight and drags down output — often by 15–25% without you realising. We restore them to like-new with 100% pure, filtered water.

  • Streak-free pure-water clean — no chemicals that void warranties or leave residue.
  • Gentle on your panels — soft brushes and the right pressure protect the glass and coating.
  • More power, lower bills — see the difference in your generation almost immediately.

Service 02

Gutter Cleaning

Clogged and overflowing gutters are the #1 cause of water damage and sagging roofs. Most Perth homes need a clean at least once a year. We clear out the leaves and debris and make sure every downpipe runs free.

  • Prevent costly damage — protect your roof, walls and foundations from water.
  • Downpipes flushed — we check the flow so the next downpour drains away properly.
  • Tidy clean-up — we bag the debris and leave your property neat.

Service 03

Pigeon & Bird Proofing

The warm, sheltered gap under solar panels is a magnet for pigeons. Their nesting brings mess, corrosive droppings, blocked gutters and even fire risk from chewed wiring. Our discreet mesh proofing seals the perimeter and keeps them out for good.

  • Long-lasting mesh barrier — UV-stable clips and mesh that blend in with your system.
  • Protects your investment — prevents damage to wiring, panels and your roof.
  • Clean before we seal — we remove existing mess so it's fresh and protected.
